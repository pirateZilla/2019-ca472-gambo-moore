About TourGo

TourGo is Ireland's first Car as a Service telematics provider. Our service combines the costs of getting on the road, spreading it across monthly installments. TourGo finds the best priced, quality vehicles and gets you behind the wheel cheaper and easier than anyone else. Included in your TourGo membership you get:

  • Your choice of vehicle from a list of brand new, top spec models.
  • Full comprehensive insurance
  • Enrollment in TourGo's Learning Platform. Become an expert driver.
  • Routine vehicle servicing and maintenance.

Who is TourGo for?

TourGo is aimed at young and new drivers. We understand that getting on the road can be very challenging for young drivers and TourGo wants to make it easier and safer for young people to get started. We've teamed up with some of Ireland's leading motor industry leaders to give you the best options, tailored to you! At present TourGo accepts drivers aged between 21 to 29. We hope to bring our services to people aged 18-21 from 2021. For drivers aged 30+ please contact our Customer Service and we'll see if we can find the right fit for you.

How is TourGo able to provide this service?

Through TourGo's learning platform, we can offer our customers highly competitive insurance and vehicles by passively improving our customer driving quality. As a TourGo member you'll receive a weekly score for your performance driving over the past 7 days. You'll then be asked to complete a brief lesson plan based on your weaker areas of driving. You'll have 7 days to complete your learning modules. What these lessons do is provide you with continuous feedback, in a casual, passive way to improve how you drive over time. TourGo promises to revolutionise the telematics leasing industry and thankfully our partners believe in this vision too.
