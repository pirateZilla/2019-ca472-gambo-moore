General - What is TourGo Insurance Cost and Billing Joining Learning Service and Repairs

TourGo is a Car as a Service platform which allows members easy access to a new affordable car throughout their subscription. We can achieve this by offering continuous passive learning. A telematics device in each TourGo vehicle gathers data on your driving behaviours and provides weekly learning to promote consistent improvements on the road. TourGo is working towards making driving safer and more accessible through modern technology and analytics.

To join, you'll first need a quote. Head over to our quote page and enter your details. Once you have your quote, you can register to purchase that policy. After agreeing to the terms and conditions and purchasing a policy you will be contacted within 48 hours by our customer service team to arrange a collection and pilot drive date.

On the collection date you'll meet your account manager at our fullfilment center. You'll be handed the keys to your new vehicle and given a briefing on how membership works. Your account manager will be able to answer any additional questions you have on the service. You'll then go on a pilot drive with your account manager to ensure you're aware of all of the vehicle's controls. The account manager will also instruct you on how to use the learning platform and maintenance checklists. This collection shouldn't take longer than an hour to complete.

Learning: As part of your contract with TourGo you are required to complete your learning platform tasks on a weekly basis. This includes the maintenance checklist. These aspects of the service are what allow us to offer competitive services. Failure to complete your learning tasks can result in termination of your account without refund.

At present we offer our service to drivers aged over 21. We intend to bring TourGo to younger drivers in the future.

Drivers aged 30+: Please contact our customer service team to arrange a personalised quote. At present our quote form only accepts users age 21-29.

TourGo's service is available to all drivers in Ireland with an Irish provisional or full licence. However we do suggest that for provisionally licenced drivers with less than one year experience should preferably be a named driver on another drivers policy prior to joining TourGo, as you'll see a significant reduction in your premium with some experience.

Non-Irish Driving Licences: For drivers with other European Union drivers licence, please contact our customer service team to arrange a personalised quote. For drivers with non-EU driver licences, we unfortunately cannot provide you with membership at this time.

Your TourGo premium is inclusive of your annual insurance for a fully comprehensive policy, where you can earn No Claims Bonus. The policy excess is €300. The class of use of this policy is for social, domestic, and pleasure. TourGo vehicles are not insured for commercial use. TourGo vehicles are insured for a maximum range of 15,000km per year. For additional kilometers please contact our customer service team for a personalised quote for an add-on.

Note: Penalties may be incurred for use of a TourGo vehicle outside of the insured use. This includes exceeding mileage or commercial use.

Your TourGo premium includes the cost of the car lease, insurance, motor tax, and annual service.

A deposit of €500 is taken at time of purchase of a TourGo policy. This deposit is then balanced against up to the final 2 months of your contract year.

TourGo provide a 14 day cooling off period to its members. Should you decide our service isn't for you within this period you will be charged a pro rata rate for the days you've used the service and refunded the remainder. For cancellations outside of this period and within contract period, a cancellation fee will be applied to the value of 2 monthly payments.

At the end of your 12 month contract you have 3 options.

  • You can continue your existing contract, with the ability to cancel with 30 days notice.
  • You can upgrade to a new vehicle, which will potential affect your monthly payment and begin a new contract period.
  • You can purchase your vehicle at a competitive market rate.

We understand that getting you on the road as soon as possible is often a top priority. As such we'll endeavour to have a collection appointment arranged within 2 weeks of payment. Your account manager will communicate more specific dates based on availability and will alert you to any expected delays.

Note: Your payment period begins from the day you receive your vehicle.

All we expect of our drivers is that they engage with the Learning Platform on a weekly basis and update their maintenance checklist as necessary.

For many young and recent drivers the extraordinary challenge and cost to getting on the road in Ireland is difficult to overcome. What TourGo offers is a way for these drivers to access a safe, reliable vehicle and to be given the tools and support to improve their driving over time.

For comparison. A 21 year old with 1 year named driving experience and a full licence could purchase a 10 year old Volskwagen Polo for €5000. The cost to insure this car annually is approximately €2500. The annual maintenace is estimated at €1000. With TourGo, this same customer can drive a brand new Volskwagen Polo for a total annual cost of €3390. TourGo have optimised the factors to getting on the road for you. We provide the best environment for you to start driving and get better.

The learning platform is effectively a large library of learning resources and lessons every time you drive your activities are assessed for quality and each journey is given a driving score. Your score is made up of a variety of different category metrics which are divided into:

  • Speed: The rate at which you comply to the speed limit.
  • Smoothness: How smooth your driving is. This is assessing your braking, acceleration, handling, as well as your control when taking corners.
  • Fatigue: How long your journeys are. Negative scoring comes into affect for journeys longer than 90 minutes without a sufficient break.
  • Time of Day: The time you drive impacts your risk of collision. As such negative scoring occurs for journeys during rush hour or overnight.

Scoring: The ideal situation for a TourGo customer is to see a consistent improvement to their driving score over time. Don't worry about achieving a 100% score, this is almost impossible. The scoring is relative and the metrics of scoring can change over time.

Unlike other telematics insurance services, TourGo's driver scores do not directly impact a driver's premium based on their insurance. TourGo's model relies on pooling our customer base together and showing that average improvements across this group justify reductions to the group premium. So you should see a change to your premium over time, but not as a direct result of your performance. This both incentivises drivers to improve while also not looming a threat of hugely inflating their premium if they don't improve.

Note: TourGo reserves the right to refuse future service to customers who are consistently scoring low or presenting suspicious driving behaviours.

For members who are having trouble improving their driving consistently, or in a particular area, TourGo will connect you with a partnered driving instructor to assist you. For a discounted price, TourGo will find a suitable instructor local to you who specialises in whatever area of driving you need to improve.

Each week, you'll be notified that you have learning platform lessons to complete and maintenance checklists to perform on your vehicle. You will have 7 days to complete the tasks from when they are issued. Failure to complete the tasks will possibly incur penalties or termination of membership. If you are unavailable to complete the tasks or need to delay a submission please notify your account manager.

The maintenance checklist serves 2 purposes. First and foremost it helps our members learn more about upkeep beyond servicing of their vehicles. Basic areas like checking the oil and other fluids are kept topped up and the tyres are in good condition are covered by the checklist. The other purpose is for you to foresee any service and maintenance requirements your car might have, as well as preventing others. Like the learning modules, the maintenance checklist is a task to be completed weekly. Any issues found should be reported via the checklist or through the help center.

Every TourGo member is covered for one routine service each year. In some cases a second service will be made available for free. Please use the service center to book in a service appointment.

Please contact us immediately and we will help you arrange the necessary insurance documentation. If an incident occured that wasn't your fault, please have all of the relevant information available and we will assist you in making a claim.

A claim for a repair outside of routine service requires payment of your excess of €300. Additional claims within a single year increase the excess to €1000. No Claims Bonus is not protected for these claims.
