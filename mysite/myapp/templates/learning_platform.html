Welcome to the Learning Platform

The learning platform is where you do your regular skills building for driving. Each week you'll be prompted to complete some lessons on the platform. Typically our platform will assess your dashboard and you'll be given lessons based on your weaker driving areas. The better your score, the fewer lessons you'll need to do.

The idea behind TourGo is passive reinforcement for driving improvements. Effectively a little bit of learning each week goes a long way to keeping you and those around you driving safely and most effectively.

Why not check out some of the lessons in our library below to get your started. Each lesson will feature some reading material, followed by a video. Then you can test yourself on the quiz below. Once you've gotten all of the answers correct on the quiz you are marked as having completed the learning requirements for the week for that lesson. Like the maintenance checklist, part of your membership requires you keep up to date with these lessons. Combined the Maintenance Checklist and lessons should take around 10 minutes each week.
